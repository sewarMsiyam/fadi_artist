Fady Abou Nasser
Illustrator and Visual Designer Specialized in Children's Books Fady Abou Nasser Illustrator | Visual Storyteller | Concept Artist I am a visual artist, born in 1986, with over ten years of experience in illustration, concept art, and digital storytelling. I have worked with many institutions worldwide, especially across the Middle East and Europe, delivering diverse works including children’s books, educational content, animated comics, and creative visual campaigns. I have collaborated with publishers in the UK, Lebanon, Kuwait, Algeria, France, and the United States. My goal is to bring stories to life with vibrant illustrations that blend emotion, culture, and imagination.
